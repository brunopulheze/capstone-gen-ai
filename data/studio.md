# NexaLab — About

## Who We Are

NexaLab is a creative and technology studio based in Amsterdam, Netherlands. We combine design thinking with engineering rigour to build digital products that are beautiful, data-informed, and technically solid.

The studio was founded by Alex Rivera — a full-stack developer and UX designer with experience spanning web development, data analytics, and AI/ML solutions. Alex has worked across industries including e-commerce, healthcare, fintech, and education.

## What We Do

We work on four interconnected disciplines:

- **AI & Chatbot Solutions** — custom language models, RAG pipelines, intelligent agents
- **Data Engineering & Analytics** — pipelines, dashboards, predictive models
- **Web Development** — full-stack applications, APIs, CMS integrations
- **Design & UX** — brand identity, UI design, accessibility audits

## How We Work

Projects begin with a scoping call to understand your goals, constraints, and timeline. We move through discovery, design, and delivery in focused sprints, keeping you involved at every checkpoint.

We work with a small roster of clients at a time — this means more focus, faster delivery, and direct communication with the person building your product.
